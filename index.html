Who is Daily Clarity designed for?

It’s built for anyone seeking more clarity, whether navigating decisions, organizing ideas, or reducing mental clutter.

Do I need journaling experience to use it?

Not at all. The experience is designed to be intuitive, guided, and accessible from the very first use.

What makes Daily Clarity different from similar products?

While most tools overwhelm you with endless features. Daily Clarity does the opposite:
it focuses on a "minimalist workflow" that forces you to prioritize what truly matters.

How can Daily Clarity help me improve my daily routine?

Daily Clarity acts as your mental compass. It helps you cut through the noise of daily tasks
by identifying your top three priorities each morning. By focusing on what truly matters and
removing non-essential distractions.

Is Daily Clarity suitable for team use or just individuals?

While Daily Clarity is primarily designed for individual use, its principles
can be applied in team settings. Teams can use it to align on priorities,
clarify goals, and foster better communication.
